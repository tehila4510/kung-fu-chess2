Kung Fu Chess — sound cues (assets/sounds/)

Files:
  select.wav     — piece clicked / selected
  deselect.wav   — selection cancelled (empty cell, off-board, reject)
  move.wav       — piece starts moving
  jump.wav       — jump in place
  capture.wav    — piece captured ("eat")
  promote.wav    — pawn promotes to queen
  game_end.wav   — king captured / game over
  game_start.wav — session / board ready
